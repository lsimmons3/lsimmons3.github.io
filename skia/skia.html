SKIA

The perfect ski companion

ACTIVITY TRACKER

The app tracks your motion data as you ski. The data is divided into three categories specific to skiing.

SKI

The ski section is broken down into time, distance, runs, speed, and carving.

AIR

The air section contains stats for air time, distance, and vertical drop.

TRICKS

Here your grabs, spins, flips, and grinds are tracked.

SOCIAL NETWORK

Skia creates a social network on the mountain. This allows you to see what's going on around you and meet new people.


LEADERBOARD

Skia also has a leaderboard for the best trick, biggest air, and best photo. By creating friendly competition the sport can be pushed from all over the world.

If a skier goes down everyone instantly gets a notification, creating a safety network on the mountain.
